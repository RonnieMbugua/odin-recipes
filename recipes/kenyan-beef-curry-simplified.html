Kenyan Beef Curry

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 pounds sirloin steak tips, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 medium red onions, finely diced
  • 4 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper or to taste
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder – mild, medium, or spicy
  • 4 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil. Add beef, garlic, and ginger. Simmer covered for 20 minutes.
  2. Drain the beef and reserve the cooking water.
  3. Sauté onions in oil until soft, about 5–7 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes. Cook until soft, 3–5 minutes.
  5. Add drained beef and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add paprika, pepper, curry powder, tomato paste, and salt. Stir well.
  7. Add reserved water and enough extra to cover. Simmer for 1 hour until thickened.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning. Garnish with fresh chilis and cilantro.
